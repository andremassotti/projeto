The Gol de Letra Foundation is a non-profit organization created by football players Raí and Leonardo. Gol de Letra operates in Rio de Janeiro and São Paulo serving children and young people through educational practices and social assistance, promoting community development. Also developed with Adobe Muse software, the Report Annual Report presents the main socio-educational projects carried out in 2016.

Project designed in collaboration with the studio Refinaria Design
